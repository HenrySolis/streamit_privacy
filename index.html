Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-16

This Privacy Policy explains how CharquiTv ("the App") collects, uses, and protects user information when accessed on Samsung Smart TVs.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely encrypted)
  • User name
  • IP address
  • Device information (TV model, operating system)
  • Application usage data (for performance and troubleshooting)

2. How We Use the Information

  • User authentication and account management
  • Providing live TV streaming and video content
  • Managing subscriptions and premium access
  • Displaying advertisements to free users
  • Improving performance, stability, and security

3. Content and Third-Party Services

CharquiTv provides content from authorized third-party providers. Some content is free, while other content requires a paid subscription. Advertisements may be shown to free users.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Data may only be shared when required by law or to protect users and the service.

5. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied to protect user data. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

6. User Rights and Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion at any time. Deleting the account permanently removes personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App may include family-friendly and children’s content. Account registration and subscriptions are intended for adults.

8. Geo-Location

CharquiTv does not apply Geo-IP blocking. Content availability may vary due to licensing agreements.
